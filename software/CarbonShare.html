CarbonShare: A Decentralized Carbon Tracking Solution

A blockchain-based solution for tracking carbon footprints, powered by Solidity, Angular, and Python.

Inspiration

The idea for this project was inspired by Yun Zhang, CEO of Mobileware, who envisioned a system that would reward users of his app, onTime Carpool, for reducing their carbon footprint. His concept involved using a digital token to quantify and verify the environmental impact of commuters.

Building on this idea, we developed CarbonShare, a decentralized solution that enables companies and individuals to track and prove their contributions to sustainability. By leveraging CarbonShare Tokens (CST), organizations can demonstrate their commitment to eco-friendly initiatives. Given the transparency and security of blockchain technology, cryptocurrency became the perfect fit for this application. We implemented CST as an ERC-20 token, making it compatible with existing DeFi applications and exchanges.
