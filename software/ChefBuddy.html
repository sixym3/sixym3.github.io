ChefBuddy

In Development

AI-powered recipe manager inspired by Paprika V3 - Smart meal planning with automated pantry tracking

Problem Statement

Existing recipe management apps, like Paprika V3, often misclassify web-scraped information and lack AI-powered features for meal planning and pantry tracking. Users must manually adjust recipes, plan meals, and track ingredients, making meal preparation tedious and error-prone.

The manual nature of these tasks creates friction in the cooking process, leading to poor meal planning, food waste from expired ingredients, and frustration with inconsistent recipe data quality.

AI-Powered Solution

ChefBuddy enhances recipe management with AI-driven automation, combining the best of traditional recipe apps with intelligent features that eliminate manual tasks.
