Carbon Share

The idea of the project thought came from Yun Zhang, the CEO of mobileware. Yun had imagined a software that would enable users of his app, onTime Carpool, to be rewarded for reducing their carbon footprint. He proposed to use some kind of token as a proof of how much carbon footprint commuter has save. We took inspiration from this idea and invented CarbonShare! By using CarbonShare Tokens, companies who wants to reduce their carbon footprint have a way to prove their involvement in eco-friendly ventures.
