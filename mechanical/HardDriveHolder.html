Custom Hard Drive Holder

Completed Q3 2021

Custom 3D-printed solution for cryptocurrency mining - Multi-drive cooling system with modular design

Project Overview

Proof-of-space cryptocurrency mining relies heavily on hard drive storage capacity and requires numerous drives running continuously. This creates significant thermal management challenges, as excessive heat can negatively impact drive performance, lifespan, and overall system stability.

During the cryptocurrency mining boom, commercial hard drive enclosures were extremely expensive and often unavailable. This project addressed the need for a cost-effective, custom solution that could accommodate multiple drives, provide effective cooling, and be easily expanded as mining operations grew.

Key Engineering Learnings

Mechanical Design & CAD Proficiency

Gained proficiency in using SolidWorks for functional mechanical design, including constraint-based modeling, assembly design, and design for manufacturability. Learned to balance structural integrity with material efficiency in 3D-printed components.

Additive Manufacturing Techniques

Developed hands-on experience with FDM 3D printing, including print orientation optimization, support structure design, and post-processing techniques. Learned material properties of various filaments and their suitability for different applications.

Thermal Management Principles

Learned fundamental principles of heat transfer and airflow design for electronics cooling. Understood the importance of strategic fan placement, air channel design, and thermal pathways for maintaining optimal operating temperatures in multi-drive systems.

System Integration & Hardware Assembly

Improved understanding of hardware integration challenges, including cable management, component spacing, and assembly sequencing. Learned to design for serviceability and future expansion while maintaining structural integrity.
