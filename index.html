About Me

A robotics engineer with a lifelong passion for integrating mechanical systems and intelligent software.

I am a robotics engineer passionate about control systems and intelligent automation. With a Master's in Mechanical Engineering, specialization in Robotics from Stony Brook University, I work at the intersection of mechanical systems and intelligent software to build precise, adaptive robotic systems for scientific and industrial applications.

My focus is on autonomous mobile robotics, particularly in developing navigation and localization systems using SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) and computer vision. I'm interested in creating robots that can perceive, navigate, and adapt to dynamic environments without human intervention.

With a strong foundation in design, analysis, and manufacturing, I'm proficient in SolidWorks with FEA optimization experience. I have hands-on experience with 3D printing, laser cutting, and CNC machining.
