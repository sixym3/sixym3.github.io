PID Turntable Control System

PID-controlled turntable system designed to accurately follow user-defined waveforms generated by a wave generator. The project successfully demonstrated the ability of both analog and digital control systems to track a target waveform, highlighting the trade-offs between the two control approaches in terms of performance and implementation complexity.

PID Control LabVIEW Analog Circuits DAQ Control Systems

Ranked 24th out of 53 teams with full STEMNaut payload recovery and comprehensive telemetry data collection. Led cross-team coordination and provided technical support across vehicle and payload systems.

RC Arduino Car

Bluetooth-controlled Arduino car with custom-designed four-bar mechanism for Ackermann steering instead of differential speed control. The project successfully demonstrated the integration of Bluetooth communication with both motor and steering control, resulting in a functional and responsive remote-controlled car with proper steering geometry.

Arduino Bluetooth C++ Mechanical Design Four-bar Mechanism
Completed Fall 2021

Custom Hard Drive Holder

Cost-effective, thermally efficient solution for housing multiple hard drives during proof-of-space cryptocurrency mining operations. The design incorporated computer case fans for active cooling to address the heat generated by the drives during operation. The project successfully resulted in a functional and efficient hard drive holder that met the specific requirements of the mining setup.

SolidWorks 3D Printing Thermal Design CAD Cooling Systems
Completed Q3 2021
