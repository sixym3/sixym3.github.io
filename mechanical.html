Mechanical Engineering Portfolio

Here are are some of my mechanical engineering related projects.

SBU Rocket Team

Our team designed, manufactured, and tested a rocket intended to reach a target altitude of 5,000 feet for the NASA Student Launch Competition. The rocket carries a STEMNaut payload, equipped with telemetry systems to gather and record flight data. All designs and procedures strictly adhere to the NASA Student Launch Handbook and its safety protocols.

RC Arduino Car

This project is a bluetooth controlled Arduino car. It uses a custom designed four bar mechanism to achieve ackermann steering. The project successfully demonstrated the integration of Bluetooth communication with both motor and steering control, resulting in a functional and responsive remote-controlled car with Ackermann steering geometry.

PID Turntable

This project focused on the design and implementation of a PID-controlled turntable capable of accurately tracking a user-defined waveform generated by a wave generator. The project successfully demonstrated the ability of both systems to track a target waveform, highlighting the trade-offs between the two approaches.

Custom Hard drive holder

This project involved the design and fabrication of a custom hard drive holder specifically for a proof-of-space cryptocurrency mining setup. The primary goal was to create a cheap, robust, and thermally efficient solution for housing multiple hard drives. The design incorporated computer case fans for active cooling to address the heat generated by the drives during operation. The project successfully resulted in a functional and efficient hard drive holder that met the specific requirements of the mining operation.
